ABOUT US

ACME BEACON SCHOOL, a school from Play group to Grade VIII at Govind Nagar, Mahmoorganj ; and Coaching institute for Classes IX to XII at Sampoornand Nagar, under the aegis of ACME Educational and Welfare Trust. ACME Beacon School is a place of excellence, which is child focused and friendly where everyone can recognize and achieve their full potential in a happy and safe environment. Our vision is to inspire every child for life through a rounded education of a child's body, mind, heart and spirit to help them grow into a complete human being. We welcome you all to be a golden link in this chain.
It is a place of belonging, where everyone is welcomed and safe. A place where learning looks exciting, talented to the needs of the children. A place that fosters relationships, where everyone is respected.

Meet The Principal

Dr. Madhumita Mitra
Principal,
ACME BEACON SCHOOL
Varanasi.

As we look forward for the future, we envision ACME BEACON SCHOOL, Varanasi as a place where excellence in education means holistic development of a child. We aim to provide the knowledge, skills, abilities, attitudes and beliefs that are essential for a productive and successful life. We see education as forming a second foundation for life-long learning. Strong communication between home and school forms the basis for the caring, nurturing family atmosphere found in our school. We seek a challenging and supportive learning environment that capitalizes on the natural curiosity of children as they explore all parts of the curriculum.
Join us in our mission to make our school a place, children love to attend, parents are happy to send and teachers express their joy of working with pride.
Warm Regards
Dr. Madhumita Mitra
